Blank Slate

You begin dancing your heart out! You dance like you’ve never danced before, a class of dance that is quite hard on the eyes. After tripping over yourself a few times you manage to find a groove that your awkward movements are able to fit. After a few minutes you hear the door open and watch a man enter and look upon your silent movements with horror and confusion.

“What are you doing?” he asks in the kindest tone he can possibly muster. “No matter, the important thing is that you seem to be awake. I am here to ask you some questions about the incident, and the person that was with you.” You interrupt your crude shuffling for a moment, he said that you came to this place with another person, but who? Would this mystery person know who you were? You knew you had to find out.

  • Dance for the audience!
  • Go with the doctor to the mystery person.
